System Design for Office Coffee Delivery Robot

Requirements:

Navigation: The robot should autonomously navigate through office spaces, avoiding obstacles and efficiently reaching the designated delivery locations.

Order Management: It should receive coffee orders from employees via a mobile app or touch screen interface, allowing them to customize their beverages (e.g., type of coffee, size, milk preference, etc.).
Beverage Preparation: The robot should be equipped with a coffee maker and ingredients to prepare a variety of coffee beverages on-demand, ensuring freshness and quality.

Delivery: It should deliver the prepared beverages to the designated desks or locations within the office, ensuring accurate and timely delivery.

User Interface: A user-friendly interface should be provided for employees to place orders, track their status, and provide feedback on the service.

Safety: The robot should adhere to safety protocols to prevent accidents and ensure the well-being of office occupants.
